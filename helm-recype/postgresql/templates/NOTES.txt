Thank you for installing the PostgreSQL chart.

To connect to your database:

  1. Get the password (if you created the secret via Helm):
     kubectl get secret {{ .Release.Name }}-postgresql -o jsonpath="{.data.DB_PASSWORD}" | base64 --decode

  2. Port-forward (local):
     kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "postgresql.fullname" . }} 5432:5432

  3. Connect with psql:
     PGPASSWORD=$(kubectl get secret {{ .Release.Name }}-postgresql -o jsonpath="{.data.DB_PASSWORD}" | base64 --decode) \
     psql -h 127.0.0.1 -p 5432 -U {{ .Values.postgres.user }} {{ .Values.postgres.database }}
